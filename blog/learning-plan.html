Learning Plan on the Bootcamp


What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal and career pathway is to become a front-end developer. I hope to use the skills I gain during Dev Academy's Bootcamp to build user-friendly and visually appealing websites and applications. My destination is to work for a company that values innovation and growth, and where I can continuously improve my skills.


What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

During Foundations, I have discovered that I have a keen eye for detail and design. However, I also realized that I am just starting with coding and have much to learn in terms of technical skills in programming.

I think my strength for detail and design will be helpful during Bootcamp, but my lack of technical skills may make things difficult. I promise to try my best to improve my technical skills and not be afraid to ask for help when I need it. Also, I want to continue to make sure my work has good detail and design to bring special ideas to my future projects.


What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I believe my biggest challenge during Bootcamp that is not about technical skills will be working with others in a group. I usually work alone and need to improve my skills for working with a team. I want to get better at working together with others and also at speaking clearly and understanding others.


What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I want to make myself better by learning leadership skills and being more conscious of myself and my actions. I also think it is important for me to get better at giving and receiving feedback, because I think these skills are very important for working well with others in a team.


What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I hope the Bootcamp team will be there to help me and give me direction throughout the program. I also want them to be there to answer any questions I have. I wish they will push me to do my best and reach my full potential by encouraging and motivating me.


What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I will try to be proactive in asking for help when I need it, and to manage my work in a way that helps me be productive and safe with others in the program. This includes other learners, teachers, and people from industry and community. I also want to make sure to take care of myself and take breaks when needed, so I can stay focused and motivated throughout Bootcamp.
