FINAL PROJECT - DEVACADEMY AOTEAROA

Buddy App

About

Introducing Buddy, the app that connects internationals to locals and helps them settle into a new country for a truly authentic cultural experience!

Moving to a new country can be intimidating, but with Buddy, you can quickly and easily connect with locals who are eager to share their culture and help you navigate your new home.

The MVP

Users can sign up for the site through the entry point, which redirects them to an authentication page. From there, they can select their user type as either international or local and register for the site.

Upon completion, they will be directed to a confirmation page. Users can also view a gallery of other users and filter the view by international or local.

Description

Over the course of a week, I collaborated with a group of eight individuals to develop a website using React.js, Typescript, Knex.js, Express.js, and Material UI. This project proved to be highly instructive, particularly with regards to the authentication process, which presented the greatest challenge.

Given additional time, I would aim to enhance the user experience by ensuring greater reliability, implementing additional features such as an email form for communication between international and local users, and bolstering security measures to authenticate legitimate users.
